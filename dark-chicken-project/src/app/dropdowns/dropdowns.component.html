DarkChicken Dropdowns



This page is designed to introduce the versatile and powerful dca-dropdown components available in the DarkChicken library for Angular developers. Our dropdown components are created to enhance user experience and expedite the development process with extensive customization options and built-in features.

Key Features

  • Various Options: DarkChicken Dropdown elements offer various options. You can enrich your projects with different options for your users to choose from.
  • Customizability: You can customize the appearance and feel of the Dropdown elements according to your projects. You can change colors, sizes, borders, and more with CSS.
  • State Management: Dropdown elements support various states such as expandable, collapsible, and selectable. These states help you manage user interactions and feedback more effectively.
  • Accessibility: DarkChicken is designed in accordance with accessibility standards. Dropdown elements are fully compatible with screen readers and keyboard navigation.
